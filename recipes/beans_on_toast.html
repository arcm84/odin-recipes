Beans on Toast

Description

This recipe describes how to make a simple beans on toast.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 can of baked beans
  • Butter (optional)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Grated cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread until golden brown.
  2. Heat the baked beans in a saucepan over medium heat.
  3. If desired, butter the toast.
  4. Pour the hot beans over the toast.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, and top with grated cheese if using.
