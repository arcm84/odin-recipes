Fishfinger Sandwich

Desciption

This recipe describes how to make a fishfinger sandwich with mayo and lettuce.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 3-4 fishfingers
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 head of lettuce
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put the fishfingers under the grill.
  2. Butter the bread.
  3. Put the fishfingers and mayo on the bread.
  4. Chop the lettuce and add that too.
