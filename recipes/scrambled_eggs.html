Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe describes how to make fluffy and creamy scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons milk or cream
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Butter
  • Chopped herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, salt, and pepper.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and add butter.
  3. Pour the egg mixture into the skillet.
  4. Stir gently with a spatula until the eggs start to set, but remain slightly runny.
  5. Remove from heat and let the residual heat finish cooking the eggs. Add herbs if desired.
